About

I am a physical oceanographer in the Department of Ocean Sciences at the Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Science of the University of Miami.

I work as a postdoctorial associate with Prof. Lisa Beal and Dr. Shane Elipot working on the Indian Ocean circulation. I am mostly working with observations, but some model output can never hurt you. I am particularly interested in the dynamics of the outflows, as I did my PhD on the Sea of Oman, Gulf of Aden and Gulf of Cadix, and in the connectivities of seasonally-evolving currents. That's my jam.

Current Projects

I am actually working on the real current pathways in the western Indian Ocean. I use both real and simulated surface drifters to understand where, when and how the main currents and large scale structures connects.
