About

I am a physical oceanographer in the Department of Ocean Sciences at the Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Science of the University of Miami.

I am mostly working with observations, but some model output can never hurt you. I am particularly interested in the dynamics of the outflows, mutli-scales from submeso to basin, and in the connectivities of seasonally-evolving currents. That's my jam.

  • 2016-2018

    Postdoctoral Associate at the University of Miami

    Study on the circulation in the western Indian Ocean using surface drifters with Lisa Beal Dr. Shane Elipot.

  • 2011-2015

    PhD at the Laboratoty of Physical and Spatial Oceanography (LOPS)

    Thesis under the direction of Prof. Xavier Carton focusing on the Arabian Sea and its adjacent gulf outflows.

  • 2006-2011

    Student in Physical Oceanography at the University of Western Britanny (UBO)

    Master degree on the Mediterranean outflow in the Gulf of Cadix.
